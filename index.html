Year: 1972

Dementia 13
Year: 1963
In Francis Ford Coppola's directorial debut for renowned genre producer Roger Corman, someone with an axe targets members of a distressed and dysfunctional family.

Beat the Devil
Year: 1953
Humphrey Bogart and a horde of less-than-moral counterparts tangle with each other over uranium while waiting for a steamer to begin the next leg of their journey. John Huston directs from a script co-written by Truman Capote.
